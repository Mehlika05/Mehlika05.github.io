chocolate cake

a delicious chocolate cake with cherries on top. Use cherries garnish!

ingredidents

  • chocolate cake ingredidents
    • 2 cup flour
    • 1½ cup cocoa powder
    • 2 eggs
    • ⅓ cup of oil
    • 1½ cup of milk
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1½ teaspoon salt

drections

  1. Peheat the oven to 350 Farenhit.
  2. place a piece of parchment on a 12 inc x 10 inc baking sheet.
  3. Prepare the cake:
  • whisk the flour, cocoa powder, eggs, baking soda, baking powder, salt in a large bowl.
  • Pour oil and egg. Stir until combined. Pour into the flour mixture and mix until batter is just combined.
